Ground layers must be pure numbers and increment by 1, starting at 0.
Item layers must be prefixed with 'i', followed by the number that indicates its layer, for example "i3" for item map on layer 3.

Ground Codes:
Stone: (77,77,77)
Dirt: (102, 82, 51)
Grass: (32, 64, 0)

Item Codes:
Plant (0, 255, 0)
